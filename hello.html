On-Demand Professional & Labor Services Web App

Problem Statement: Finding trusted professionals (advocates, tutors, electricians, doctors, etc.) is time-consuming. No single platform covers both professional and labor services. Issues with pricing transparency, service reliability, and availability.

Our Solution: A web app to connect users with verified professionals & laborers. Features include:

  • Instant booking
  • Real-time availability
  • Transparent pricing
  • AI-based recommendations for a better user experience
  • Customer reviews and ratings to ensure quality service

Revenue Model:

  • Commission-based: Small percentage per transaction
  • Subscription Model: Premium visibility for professionals
  • Ads & Partnerships: Featured listings and corporate tie-ups

Market Potential: Growing gig economy with increasing demand for home and professional services.

Tech Stack & Next Steps:

  • Frontend: React.js / Angular
  • Backend: Node.js / Django
  • Database: Firebase / PostgreSQL
  • AI Features: Recommendation system for personalized service selection

Next Steps: MVP development & user testing, partnering with service providers, and launching a beta version.
